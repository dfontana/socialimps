Notes on the Movie

Synopsis and major characters (IMDb):

In near-future London, police detective Karin Parke, and her tech-savvy sidekick Blue, investigate a string of mysterious deaths with a sinister link to social media.

  • Karin Parke - DCI?
  • Blue Colson - shadowing Parke, used to work for “tech crime”
  • Shaun Li - National Crime Agency officer
  • Rasmus Sjolberg - head of the ADI program
  • Garrett Scholes - behind #DeathTo, former employee working on ADI

0:00

  • Black Mirror title: cracked screen - the name “Black Mirror” refers to screens
  • UK Setting, May 15th
  • Door was a keypad, not key.
  • Apartment has everything at a remote control: shades, kitchen. Smart home? Subtle.
  • News announcement: another extinction, the Siberian Crane
  • Honey Bees that are drones, second summer. They are replacements to the honey bee extinction.
  • Jo Powers? Joan?
    • Security camera over her front door (red head)
    • She is apparently hated :S Wrote a bad article about some martyr?
    • Wrote article critical of “wheel chair” martyr who set self on fire to protest cuts in disability benefits.
    • 20,000 people sign petition calling for her to be fired
    • Her computer monitor has some seriously slim bezels and a lot of pings and pangs for notifications.
    • Notifications: insults, profanity, death wishes

6:45 mins in

  • Karen is the brown haired one. Blonde haired on is “blue”
  • Jo Powers dead.
    • Her clickbait piece spawned a lot of hatred.
    • Her neck was slit real bad. Like real bad.
    • Husband was attacked too - but not ded.
    • Her tweets keep streaming in :S So loud. Obnoxious

  • e-Cig from Karen. Blue was in “tech-crime”. Digital forensics.
    • Blue - “Phones absorb us. Know everything we are.”
    • She saw some pretty bad photos and videos of some digital crime. Either defeated or think “fuck me” we have to stop this.
    • “Out here in the real world you can generally prevent stuff can’t ya” - Blue. “You are young” - Karen. “Maybe you’re old” - Karen.

13 mins in.

  • Blue searches for Jo’s username on twitter, using keywords (aggressive ones). Does a substring match on all tweets. Advanced search for a social networking site…

15 mins

  • “Half hate”…
  • “That internet hate don’t mean anything its like weather. They hate in a marriage, that’s 3D” - Karen.
  • “Waiting for driver-less to go mainstream”

20 mins

  • That phone bezel tho. Can just search up her social feeds. Phone is see through.
  • Daycare Worker:
    • “Death to JoPowers” was her tweet.
    • “I was just using my freedom of speech” - daycare worker? Who said something nasty on social media. Denies wanting her dead. Her twitter begged to differ. Then she said it was a joke, it doesn’t mean anything…
    • Offensive Communication Act - Cake writer in trouble now.

23 mins

  • Karen - Arresting the husband, dismissing the internet. “Don’t worry about it, they’ll be mad at something else today”

~24 mins

  • Tusk attacked, ADI pulled out through eye during MRI, killing him

26 mins

  • Bee discovered as the killer. “ADI” - Autonomous Drone Insect.

29 Mins

  • High-tech company bankrolled by the Gov’t to build the ADI’s. They are paranoids the Goven’t are spying on them with it. Statue in the lobby are a bunch of the ADIs working in unison. Kinda like the olympics at Korea.

30 - 34 mins

  • ADIs
    • Colony Collapse Disorder - they don’t know what’s behind it, so they built the ADIs as a means to fill in the gaps
    • They are autonomous with no control - they set the behavior and go for it
    • They reproduce - they build hives themselves where they act as 3D printers to make more
    • They have a rudimentary vision sensor to help navigate
    • “These are not designed to kill someone” “When they malfunction they just break and hit the ground”
    • To hack it you’d need a diagnostic tool that’s encrypted and its got military grade encryption.
    • They can see every hive they have online - so they know where the hives are
    • 4,000 per hive. It’s like an aircraft control.
    • Bees pathing is logged, can replay.
    • Has to be within a 20 meter radius
    • Can swap phone numbers by tapping phones
    • Can’t shutdown the bees within environment issues and needs approval from Gov’t.
    • CEO of business highly concerned and asks for them to leave. He recalls the bees to their hives????
    • When leaving, Blue and Rasmus trade contact info by touching phones - has already been tried, didn’t catch on

37 Mins

  • “No fingerprints, no DNA, it’s like wishing them dead” - Karen
  • Blue’s laptop has a touchscreen keyboard, see through screen.
  • HashTag sent from bot account started the murder tweet - seed the tag. They wanted people to pickup on it.
  • “Game of Consequences” - pick someone you don’t like, pick enough and that’s who they target.

41 Mins

  • Pulling citizens details - can look up someone’s location. You’re not supposed to have access to it, but bleu does. Gov’t employee abuse.
    • Next victim watching the tweet roll in, freaking out.
    • Her roommate has no idea how the police have her number.
    • Car door has remotes to open it. Touch screen controller. Seats roll around
    • Can turn all lights green in their path for the super car. Its auto pilot, just got to tell it where to go on
the iPad.

46 Mins

  • CEO running tracer on it the rogue bee.
  • Bee followed to house on their car
  • Can’t trace it - Ramsus (CEO guy)… Whole hive just went down. Thousands of them. Close to their location.
  • Their phones are being jammed. Entire colony has been taken over. Across the entire country.
  • Huge death swarm inbound.
  • Trapped inside the bath. The bees are working together to get through the door & vents.
  • Killed the victim, now they sit in the bathroom surrounded unsure of what is to come next.
  • Goes up nose, not through ear this time
  • In person death changed Blue - more driven now

55 Mins

  • Turns out it’s not a radius, someone can control the entire system.
  • The ID Database gives the basic area, but how did they know who to target?
  • Facial recognition is built in
  • The government forced the company to let a backdoor in so they could watch surveil the entire public.
    • It protected the public by spying on suspects
    • Saw a chance for more, took it
    • “Routed through GCHQ”

60 Mins

  • People know they are dying but people are still taking part - they are getting insane over it
  • Since people can jump on board without anyone knowing they did it - they do it. Anonymity.
  • People question whether it is legitimate, how culpable they are
  • “Someone is going to die, might as well make it someone who deserves it”
  • News is reporting it like it’s a “top5 race”
  • Chancellor: Shut the whole media down - the whole internet - pull a North Korea on it.
    • He wants to leak a doc to throw someone under the bus in front of him.

1:04

  • Victim brought in for questioning
  • It was like a weather system circling over you, 1 million invisible people talking about how they despise you. Like a mental illness. The way people enjoyed kicking / fun they had. Felt like she couldn’t go on. Tried to kill herself. Someone found her and saved her
    • The one who saved her - Garret. He put a manifesto in ‘the drones “the teeth of consequences”

1:08

  • After attempting to blow up a hive, the bees attack the soldiers and kill them.
  • Ecological disaster if the bees go away, controlling murders if kept around.
  • Garrett - “Thanks to the technologal revolution we have the power to rage and accuse, but bile without consequence. Only if we recognize the power…” … theme is he wants people to face the consequences of what they say and do.
    • Immediately one of the agents goes and tweets #DeathTo Garrett and posts his image. Hesitates on doing it, but then does it. Playing right into the point…
  • Phone left a geotag on the selfie he took. They found out he is outside granular HQ
  • Garrett learns the police are after him due to the agent’s tweet…….
    • He gets away….

1:13

  • Burned a harddrive in a forge. Lets Ramsus get back into the system.
  • USBs connect via magnets.
  • IMEI - unique user ID. The drive contains a list of everyone who has used the HashTag. The Backdoor let them get the list of details on the people from the government’s DB.
  • Karen - what if the list is the real targets, those who took part. The players are the targets. “Consequences.”
  • The NSA guy runs the deactivate script, supposed to regain control. But it’s not a recontrol script. It’s the trap.

1:17

  • The hive mobilizes… In position for mass murder.
  • Garrett is somewhere remote, packing? Stripping. Shaving completely? Changes his eye color with contacts. Dumps his stuff into a lake after filling it with rocks.

1:22

  • Aftermath. Warehouse. Just a huge warehouse of the dead.
  • Blue blamed herself, felt she sprung it all. Left the force, went MIA, and found her things 4 months later on a beach with a note. She had committed suicide - in theory.
  • Garrett watching the hearings from a coffee shop? Spanish country
  • Blue not dead, but tracked down garrett. Suicide was staged.

1:27

  • Ends with Blue tailing garret.
